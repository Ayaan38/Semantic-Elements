Facts about dogs

Dogs are domesticated mammals,They are common pets but can be dangerous as well. They are classified as a subspecies of Canis lupus, along with wolves and dingoes. Dogs were domesticated from wolves over 14,000 years ago

Dogs are loyal companions. They enjoy spending time with their owners and can be trained to perform various tasks.
